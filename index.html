The Constant Thudding of Mass Shootings

  • Though all the more common,
  • and increasingly severe,
  • shootings are background noise -
  • a constant thudding we forget.
  • Each time, without fail,
  • the questions arises:
  • How could this happen?
  • Some focus on mental health.
  • Surely others noticed?
  • But the answer is rarely that clear.
  • Others look to the weapons.
  • How did they get them?
  • Though clearer, the question remains.
  • How can we stop this from happening again?
  • Though clear trends emerged,
  • nothing has been done.
  • Without meaningful change,
  • the constant thudding of
  • unceasing, senseless shootings
  • will remain in the background.
